<section class="section two-column">
<div class="readable">
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Orthodox connect is an in-development platform for parishes, dioceses, and jurisdictions to securely communicate.</p>
<p>It includes a community portal, XMPP-based chat, group and community spaces, library, and online video conferencing. Private where required, and suitable for parish, monastery, diocesan, or local community use where authorised.</p>
</div>
<aside class="notice">
<p class="badge">Federated Communities</p>
<p>Orthodox Connect can connect communities, parishes, and dioceses in a federation that allows inter-communication between them as allowed by administrators.</p>
</aside>
</section>
