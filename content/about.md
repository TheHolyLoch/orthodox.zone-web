<h2>Purpose</h2>
<p>Orthodox Zone is a simple website for Orthodox Christian information and related software projects. It is intended to be useful to readers in Britain, Scotland, Ireland, the USA, and the wider English-speaking world.</p>
<p>Run by Holy Loch Media, we also produce original content, publications, and broadcasts.</p>
<p>We are not an official Church body, parish, diocese, or canonical authority. Please check with your local Orthodox Priest before using any of the resources here.</p>
</div>
