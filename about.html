About

A public hub for Orthodox Christian resources.

Orthodox Zone gathers project links, calendar access, community tools, and future educational material in one simple place.

Purpose

Orthodox Zone is a static-first website for Orthodox Christian information and related software projects. It is intended to be useful to readers in Britain, Scotland, and the wider English-speaking world while keeping a modest tone.

The site can point to community tools such as Orthodox Connect, calendar work through Orthocal, and future articles or guides. It does not present itself as an official Church body, parish, diocese, or canonical authority.

Content should remain respectful, checked, and clearly marked when it is only a placeholder. Public material can be expanded later with proper review.

Focus areas

  • Orthodox Christian basics
  • Prayer and worship resources
  • Calendar access and API links
  • British and Celtic saints
  • Community and communication tools
