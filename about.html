About

A public hub for Orthodox Christian resources.

Orthodox Zone gathers project links, calendar access, community tools, and educational material in one simple place.

Purpose

Orthodox Zone is a simple website for Orthodox Christian information and related software projects. It is intended to be useful to readers in Britain, Scotland, Ireland, the USA, and the wider English-speaking world.

Run by Holy Loch Media, we also produce original content, publications, and broadcasts.

We are not an official Church body, parish, diocese, or canonical authority. Please check with your local Orthodox Priest before using any of the resources here.

Focus areas

  • Orthodox Christian basics
  • Prayer and worship resources
  • Calendar access and API links
  • British and Celtic saints
  • Community and communication tools
