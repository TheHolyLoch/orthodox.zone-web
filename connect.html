Orthodox Connect

A secure Orthodox community platform for portal access, chat, rooms, and future meeting tools.

Project role

Orthodox Connect is presented here as a related community platform for Orthodox groups that need structured access, communication, and community spaces.

The expected areas include a community portal, chat, XMPP, group and community spaces, and a placeholder path for meet or video integration. These areas should remain clear, private where required, and suitable for parish, monastery, diocesan, or local community use where authorised.
