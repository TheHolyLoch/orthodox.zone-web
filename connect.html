Upcoming Community platform

Orthodox Connect

A secure Orthodox community platform for portal / library access, chat rooms, and meeting tools.

Overview

Orthodox connect is an in-development platform for parishes, dioceses, and jurisdictions to securely communicate.

It includes a community portal, XMPP-based chat, group and community spaces, library, and online video conferencing. Private where required, and suitable for parish, monastery, diocesan, or local community use where authorised.

Federated Communities

Orthodox Connect can connect communities, parishes, and dioceses in a federation that allows inter-communication between them as allowed by administrators.
